About Edmond

A Mechanic
Who Works For
You.

"He will never take advantage of you and will never charge you more."

Edmond — the founder of Auto-X Repair — built this business on a simple idea: honest mechanics shouldn't be hard to find. He comes to your car wherever it is, shows you exactly what's wrong, and never upsells you on parts you don't need.

Serving the greater Los Angeles and Burbank area, Edmond has built a reputation as a reliable neighbor mechanic — the kind you keep in your phone for life. From quick brake jobs to dead engines brought back from the brink, he handles it all on-site with patience and precision.
